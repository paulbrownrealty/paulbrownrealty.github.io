Expertise in Real Estate

Hi, there! My name's Paul. I'm a real estate agent.

I set up this website to introduce myself and to tell you about what I have to offer.

A lot goes into a real estate transaction. There's marketing, negotiating, inspections, financing, appraising value, title work, and the list goes on. I represent buyers and sellers to protect their interests. I let them know what they're required to disclose and help them keep their personal information private. I make sure that contingencies are in place so that the people I represent don't wind up stuck in a bad contract that they can't get out of. On behalf of my clients, I negotiate and coordinate with other buyers, sellers, real estate agents, title agents, lenders, appraisers, inspectors, and more. I watch over the different moving parts of the deal to help see that everything is done right and on time. All the while, I keep my clients in the loop and I don't do anything without their approval. I'm there for them from beginning to end and then some.

If you decide to work with me, I'll prepare you for and guide you through this process.
